Pudding Poke Cake

This cake is known for having holes poked into the sponge, which are then filled with pudding, creating a moist and delicious dessert.

1. Description

This cake is an excellent example of a dessert that is perfect for those who are short on time. It is fast to make and the best thing is - everyone can make it. It is attractive and it leaves impression that it took knowledge and hard work to make. And lots of time, of course.

2. Ingredients

Base:

  • 6 eggs, at room temperature
  • 120 g granulated sugar
  • 1 vanilla sugar
  • 150 ml sunflower oil
  • 150 ml whole cow's milk
  • 100 g fine flour
  • 60 g cocoa powder
  • 1 baking powder

Yellow cream

  • 2 vanilla pudding powder
  • 100 g granulated sugar
  • 900 ml whole cow's milk
  • 125 g butter

Dark cream

  • 2 chocolate pudding powder
  • 100 g granulated sugar
  • 900 ml whole cow's milk
  • 125 g butter
  • 200 g chocolate*

Decoration

  • 250 ml whipped cream
  • shaved chocolate
